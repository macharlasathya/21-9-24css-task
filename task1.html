Meta tags are HTML tags placed within the section of a webpage that provide metadata about the document. They do not appear in the content ofthe page but are used by browsers,search engines, and other services to understand the page's characteristics.

what are the iframes?

IFrames (Inline Frames) are HTML elements used to embed another HTML document within the current document. They allow you to display content from another source, such as another webpage, video, or application, directly on your page.

what is the advantage of collapsing in whitespace?

IFrames are powerful tools for embedding external content within a webpage, allowing for a richer user experience. However, they should be used carefully, considering potential security implications.

what are the html entities?

HTML entities are special codes used to represent characters that have a specific meaning in HTML or that cannot be easily typed. They ensure that the desired characters are displayed correctly in web browsers, especially for characters that may otherwise be interpreted as HTML syntax.
